Chicken Alfredo

Description

A creamy chicken alfredo with broccoli :)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of Penne Pasta
  • 1 cup of chopped broccoli
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • ground black pepper to liking
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan Cheese

    • Steps

    1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add the pasta and let it cook until al dente
    2. Cut the chicken breasts into sizes of your liking. In a skillet add the minced garlic and butter on medium heat, then add the chicken and saute until brown.
    3. Strain and rinse the pasta, after you add the milk and cheese to the skillet also add the pasta and broccoli and allow it to cook to your liking.
