Brownies

Description

Chocolaty walnut brownies! :]

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1 cup of flower
  • 2/3 cup of butter
  • 2/4 cup of chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees, and then butter a 9x9 square pan
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a pan and stir until smooth. In a bowl, beat the eggs and salt with a mixer, then add the sugar, vanilla, and the chocolate and beat on on high until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until completely smooth, then add the walnuts and mix.
  4. Pour the batter into the greased pan, then place in the oven. Bake for 40 minutes and then let cool.
