Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

Homemade strawberry ice cream ^_^

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries, cut
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, aadd the milk, strawberries, cream, vanilla, salt, and sugar. Mix and pour into the freezer bowl of the ice cream maker.
  2. Freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.
